The Hikes

There are a variety of hiking options in the Maze. Trailheads are near the 4WD camp sites.

Chocolate Drops

easy

Chocolate Drops Trail is a 7.3 mile lightly trafficked out and back trail located near 4WD site The Wall. It features beautiful wild flowers and is good for all skill levels. The trail is primarily used for hiking, walking, running, and nature trips and is accessible year-round. It is mostly level and follows the top of a sandstone ridge. The Harvest Scene is just below, but you cannot reach it from the ridge.
Link to AllTrails

Spanish Bottom

hard

Spanish Bottom is a 2.8 mile lightly trafficked out and back trail located near The Doll House. This hike is all down and then all up to get back to the Doll House.
Link to AllTrails

Colorado/Green River Overlook

moderate

Moderate trail. There is little shade. The trail leads through snady wash bottoms, across slickrock tableland, and meadows to spectacular views of the Colorado and Green Rivers. It is 5 miles oneway.
Link to AllTrails

Harvest Scene

hard

Harvest Scene is a 9.3 mile moderately trafficked loop trail located near Chimney Rock 4WD stie. It offers scenic views and is rated as difficult. The trail is primarily used for hiking and nature trips and is accessible year-round. This is a tough hike, especially in the hot sun.
Link to AllTrails
