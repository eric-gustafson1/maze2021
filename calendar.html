Trip Calendar

This is a best effort day by day break down. All 3 4WD camp sites are relatively close. Daily activities are suggested based on their proximity to that day's camp.

Sunday, May 30

Camp North of Goblin Valley

Leave Denver and head towards Goblin Valley. Camp north of Goblin Valley. Look for dispersed site off Temple Mountain Road west of UT24, north of Hanksville. Maybe try to find dinner in Green River or Hanksville.

Monday, May 31

Camp at The Wall

It is about 5 hour drive from near Hanksville to The Wall. We need to pick up the backcountry permit by 2:30PM. From The Wall we can hike Chocolate Drops.

Tuesday, June 1

Camp at Doll House 2

Doll House 2 is near the Colorado River canyon rim. Hiking options down to the Colorado river on the Spanich Bottom Trail and to an overlook of the Colorado and the Green rivers.

Wednesday, June 2

Camp at Doll House 2

Chance to make one of the hikes we haven't done yet. There are hikes along the rim in both directions.

Thursday, June 3

Camp at Standing Rock

This is a favorite 4WD site. This site is at the rim of the Maze. Chances to hike down into the Maze.

Friday, June 4

Camp at Standing Rock

The trailhead to hike to the Harvest Scene is just down the road at Chimney Rock. This is a long tough hike but really worth it. To come this far and not hike to Harvest would be a mistake. Leave early to avoid some heat mid-day.

Saturday, June 5

Camp at Standing Rock

This is a great spot to hike into the Maze. Sunsets are fantastic.

Sunday, June 3

Head Home

Get up and start the drive home. Get an early start so we have time for breaks/meals.
